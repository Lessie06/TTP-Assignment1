Why is beach pollution even a problem?

Swimming or being in contact with polluted beaches poses serious health risks.
Remember that polluted beaches affect both the sand and water at the beach so practice
caution anytime you see a polluted beach. The EPA estimates that up to 3.5 million get
sick from being in contact with sewage while swimming. This often leads to an upset stomach
but can lead to neurologicall disorders, hepitatis, and meningitits.

Not only that but polluted beaches also destroys the habitats of several aninals.
This causes them to move other enviroments they may not be adapted to live in and
leaves the place open to oppurtunistic animals who may find the new trash ridden place
home. In any case whether it be for personal reasons or enviromental ones. Its important
to preserve our beaches.
